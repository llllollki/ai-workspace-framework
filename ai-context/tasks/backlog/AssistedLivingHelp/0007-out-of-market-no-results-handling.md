# Define Out-of-Market and No-Results Handling

Status: backlog
Created: 2026-05-04
Owner role: Product / Program Lead
Reviewers: Operations / Concierge Workflow Lead, Compliance / Privacy Counsel

## Goal

Define what the platform should do when a family is outside the Phase 1 markets or when dependable matching returns no strong facility results.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Out-of-market intake behavior is defined.
- No-results and weak-match family messaging is defined.
- Internal staff workflow for manual review/escalation is defined.
- The system avoids implying that unsupported areas are actively covered.
- Follow-up implementation impact is identified for intake, matching, confirmation, and admin workflows.
