# alh-tracker — Design Partner Candidate Tracker

**Created:** 2026-05-10
**Purpose:** Candidate list and outreach status tracker for the design partner search (Task 0002).
**Owner action required:** Build the warm-list section from ALH contact data. Verify all registry rows against the live CCLD portal. Do not rely on third-party directory data for final capacity or license status.

---

> **How to use this tracker:**
> - Section A: Warm list — ALH contacts. Owner must populate from ALH CRM or contact list.
> - Section B: Registry candidates — pre-seeded from public third-party directories (not CCLD direct). Verify each against the CCLD facility search before outreach.
> - Section C: Adjacent geography. Activate only if Sections A and B do not yield a committed partner in 4–6 weeks.
> - Priority scoring rules: 1 = highest priority. See scoring guide below the table.
> - Do not contact a facility until its license status is confirmed active in CCLD.
> - Do not contact any facility that is a known PointClickCare / MatrixCare user.

> No outreach has been conducted. Section A (warm list) is not yet filled. Section B/C rows are pre-seeded from third-party directories and require CCLD verification before any contact is made.

**Run these steps in order:**

**Step 1 — Fill Section A from ALH CRM (30–60 min)**
Open ALH facility partner or prospective contact list. Filter: RCFE license type, Temecula/Murrieta/Menifee/SW Riverside County, capacity 6–20. Add each match to Section A with owner contact info and known ALH relationship. Any ALH contact who also appears in Section B/C upgrades to Priority 1 or 2 — cross-reference and merge.

**Step 2 — Verify top-priority Section B rows in CCLD (1–2 hrs)**

**Step 3 — Score and rank**
After filling Section A and verifying Section B capacity, apply the scoring guide below. Move Priority 1 and 2 rows to the top of your outreach order.

**Step 4 — Send first outreach message (one at a time)**

- Send one message per facility. Wait 5 business days before a single follow-up. Follow up once only.

**Step 5 — When a facility says yes**
Schedule the site visit. Confirm the LOI outline (task 0002, Section 5) or a verbal equivalent before visiting. Run the visit using the site visit plan (task 0002, Section 4) and the on-site observation checklist (task 0002, Section 4b).

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1 | Existing ALH partner + Temecula/Murrieta/Menifee + capacity 10–16 |
| 2 | Existing ALH partner + preferred geography + capacity outside 10–16; OR registry-only + preferred geography + capacity 10–16 |
| 3 | Registry-only + Temecula/Murrieta/Menifee + capacity 6–9 or 17–20 |
| 4 | Any facility in adjacent SW Riverside County city (Hemet, Lake Elsinore, Wildomar, Canyon Lake) |

> **Observation (2026-05-10):** The Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee RCFE market is dominated by 6-bed homes (the California RCFE standard license size for residential homes). No facilities with capacity 10–16 were identified in publicly accessible third-party directories. This means most cold-list candidates will score Priority 3 unless they are ALH partners (Priority 2) or a 10–16 capacity facility is found via CCLD direct search. The ALH warm contact list is therefore the most likely source of Priority 1 and 2 candidates. Owner should check the CCLD search directly for any RCFE licensed at capacity 7–20 in these cities — these may not appear on third-party referral directories.

## How to Complete This Tracker

### Step 1 — Owner fills Section A (estimated 2–4 hours)

1. Open ALH facility partner contact list or CRM.
2. Filter: RCFE license type, Temecula/Murrieta/Menifee/SW Riverside County, capacity 6–20.
3. Add each match to Section A above: name, city, capacity, ALH tier, owner contact, known disqualifiers.
4. Cross-reference with Section B and C rows — if a registry-listed facility is also an ALH contact, upgrade it to Section A (warm) and adjust priority score.

### Step 2 — CCLD verification (estimated 1–2 hours)

1. Go to: **https://www.ccld.dss.ca.gov/carefacilitysearch/**
2. For each Section B / C facility: confirm license status = Licensed (active), confirm capacity.
3. Update capacity column. Remove any facility with inactive license or capacity < 6.
4. Remove any facility known to use PointClickCare, MatrixCare, or similar.

### Step 3 — Score and rank

After filling in ALH relationship and confirmed CCLD capacity, apply scoring guide above. Move Priority 1 and 2 facilities to the top of the outreach order.

### Step 4 — Outreach execution

- Contact Priority 1 and 2 first. Contact in strict priority order: one at a time.
- Use warm-introduction email for ALH contacts (task 0002, Section 3).
- Use cold phone opener for registry contacts (task 0002, Section 3).
- Wait 5 business days before a single follow-up. Follow up once only.
